·ABOUT

    This website is designed to assist students in visualizing their academic data through tables or facilitating the calculation of their GPA. It is important to clarify that there are no malicious intentions behind the use of student data; the sole purpose is to provide a platform for 2021 year admission students. Any issues or concerns related to students from other admission years are not considered.

    If you wish to refrain from having your grades or name shared, please feel free to contact me. By utilizing the services of this website, you implicitly accept the terms outlined in the privacy policy and terms of use.

·RANKOMETER

    RANKOMETER is a tool for visualizing academic standing, tailored for the 2021 admission cohort. We handle student data with utmost privacy. Our user-friendly interface displays key metrics such as rank, identification, name, current GPA, success percentage, and ranking changes. No malicious intent is involved in accessing data; our goal is to enhance user understanding. If you notice any discrepancies in your data, please contact us  promptly for resolution.

·FENOMETER

    FENOMETER is a dedicated section designed to provide a comprehensive overview of academic performance, focusing on individual subjects across various faculties and semesters. Offering a detailed recap of each semester, this tool enables students to visualize their subject grades separately, facilitating a nuanced understanding of their academic journey. Additionally, FENOMETER highlights students participating in summer school programs in France. Our commitment to privacy and transparency remains steadfast, and we encourage users to explore this feature for an enriched perspective on their educational endeavors. If you have any questions or encounter discrepancies in your data, please don't hesitate to send us a message for prompt resolution.
