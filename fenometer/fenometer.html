FENOMETER

·FENOMETER

    FENOMETER is a dedicated section designed to provide a comprehensive overview of academic performance, focusing on individual subjects across various faculties and semesters. Offering a detailed recap of each semester, this tool enables students to visualize their subject grades separately, facilitating a nuanced understanding of their academic journey. Additionally, FENOMETER highlights students participating in summer school programs in France. Our commitment to privacy and transparency remains steadfast, and we encourage users to explore this feature for an enriched perspective on their educational endeavors. If you have any questions or encounter discrepancies in your data, please don't hesitate to send us a message for prompt resolution.
