RANKOMETER

· ALL TIME PE


This section of the Rankometer is specifically designed to monitor and display the changes in students' GPAs over time, as well as their corresponding rank. You may choose to view data from four distinct faculties listed below, or opt for the "ALL" selection to display the students currently enrolled in the fourth year (L3) for the year 2025. By clicking on any student row in the table, you will be able to expand the details and view the GPA values for all semesters, along with the average GPA for the respective programs.

The rank is determined based on the student's cumulative GPA over the entire four years of study. The coefficient for semesters L0S1 and L0S2 is 20, while each subsequent semester is assigned a coefficient of 30.

Please note that there may be minor discrepancies between the GPA displayed in the table and the official transcript of the student. Therefore, it is advisable to rely primarily on the transcript for the most accurate information.
