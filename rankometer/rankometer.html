RANKOMETER

·RANKOMETER

    RANKOMETER is a tool for visualizing academic standing, tailored for the 2021 admission cohort. We handle student data with utmost privacy. Our user-friendly interface displays key metrics such as rank, identification, name, current GPA, success percentage, and ranking changes. No malicious intent is involved in accessing data; our goal is to enhance user understanding. If you notice any discrepancies in your data, please contact us  promptly for resolution.
